📝 Reporting an Issue on LaporFix

Reporting a problem in your community is quick and easy. Follow these steps to ensure your report reaches the right department:

1. Open the Report Form
Click on the (+) icon on your dashboard to open the submission form.

2. Capture and Upload
Start by uploading a clear photo of the issue. A good visual helps our team assess the severity and bring the right tools for the fix.

3. Categorize Your Report
Select the appropriate category (e.g., Road Damage, Waste Management, Street Lighting). This ensures the report is routed to the correct specialized team.

4. Set the Location
Click "Next" to access the map.
GPS Pin: Your current GPS location will be detected automatically. Please ensure the pin is dropped exactly where the issue is located.
Address Details: Add any specific landmarks or floor numbers in the additional address field to help our team find the spot faster.

5. Finalize with Notes
Add any additional notes or descriptions (e.g., "The pothole is hidden under a puddle"). Once you're ready, hit Submit.

💡 What happens next?
You will receive a Ticket ID immediately. You can track the progress of your fix in real-time through the "My Reports" section or by asking LAPI for an update!

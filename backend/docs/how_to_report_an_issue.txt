📝 Reporting an Issue on LaporFix

Reporting a problem in your community takes just a few steps. Here is exactly how the process works:

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — Open the Report Form
─────────────────────────────────────────
Tap the (+) button on the Issue tab to open the "Create Report" screen.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 2 — Upload a Photo
─────────────────────────────────────────
Tap the photo area and select an image from your device gallery.
- Tap "Add Photo" to upload for the first time.
- Once uploaded, tap "Edit Photo" to replace it, or tap the (×) icon in the
  top-right corner of the image to remove it.

A photo is required before you can proceed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 3 — Select a Category
─────────────────────────────────────────
Choose the category that best describes the issue from the dropdown:

  • Pothole
  • Broken Street Light
  • Power Outage
  • Water Leakage
  • Road Damage
  • Traffic Light Problem
  • Garbage Overflow
  • Other

If you select "Other", a text field will appear where you can type a custom
category name (maximum 12 characters).

A category is required before you can proceed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 4 — Add a Description
─────────────────────────────────────────
Type a description of the issue in the text field provided. Be as specific as
possible — for example: "Large pothole near the junction, partially hidden by
standing water."

A description is required before you can proceed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 5 — Tap "Next" to Set the Location
─────────────────────────────────────────
Tapping "Next" will:
1. Validate that a photo, category, and description have all been filled in.
2. Fetch your current GPS location automatically (a loading spinner will
   appear briefly).
3. Open the map screen with a pin placed at your current location.

On the map screen:
- A red location pin is displayed at the centre of the map — this marks the
  reported location.
- Drag the map to reposition the pin to the exact spot of the issue.
- Use the search bar at the top to search for an address by name. Suggestions
  are provided by OpenStreetMap Nominatim. Tap a suggestion to jump the map
  to that location.
- Tap the (×) icon in the search bar to clear the current address text.

Once the pin is correctly placed, tap "Next" to proceed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 6 — Add Address Details and Notes
─────────────────────────────────────────
After confirming the map location, a form slides up with two optional fields:

  Address Details (Optional)
    Additional location information such as block number, unit number, or
    floor — e.g., "Block A, Unit 12".

  Additional Notes (Optional)
    Any extra context that may help the team — e.g., "The streetlight only
    fails after 10 PM" or "Issue is near the back entrance".

Tap the back arrow to return to the map and adjust the pin if needed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 7 — Submit the Report
─────────────────────────────────────────
Tap "Submit Report". The app will:
1. Upload the photo to Firebase Storage.
2. Save the report to Firestore under the "issue" collection with a status
   of "submitted".

A success dialog will appear once the report is saved. Tap anywhere on it
to dismiss and return to the home screen.

─────────────────────────────────────────
💡 What happens next?
─────────────────────────────────────────
Your report is immediately visible in the Issue tab (Status Tracker), where
you can follow its progress through the following statuses:

  submitted → under review → in progress → resolved

You can also ask LAPI (the AI chatbot) for an update on your report at any
time.

# LaporFix Chatbot (LAPI) — User Guide

## Overview

LAPI is the AI assistant inside the LaporFix app. It helps Malaysian citizens ask questions about reporting urban issues (potholes, water cuts, power outages, drainage, road works, and more).

LAPI uses a knowledge base (app help docs and government sources) plus your chat history to answer. Replies are kept short — under 60 words, in complete sentences.

You must be signed in for chat history to be saved. History is stored in Firestore under your user account.

---

## How to Open LAPI

1. Sign in to LaporFix.
2. Tap the **LAPI** floating button (robot icon) on the main screens.
3. The chat screen opens with a greeting and three suggestion cards.

---

## Welcome Suggestion Cards

On first open, three quick-action cards appear:

| Card | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **How to report an issue?** | Asks LAPI how to file a report. The reply includes a **Report Issue** button that opens the issue reporting form. |
| **Track my existing ticket** | Looks up your open tickets in the app (Submitted / In Progress). Does not use the AI — reads your Firestore issues directly. |
| **Check for water/power cut** | Searches announcements in your area for water, power, or road maintenance. Shows styled **disruption notice cards** (water cut, power cut, or road maintenance) when a match is found. |

You can also type your own question instead of using a card.

---

## Sending Messages

### Plain text

- Type in the box at the bottom and tap send.
- LAPI remembers the last few messages in the same session for follow-up questions.

### Image upload

1. Tap **+** next to the input box.
2. Choose **Upload Image**.
3. Optionally add a text question (e.g. "What is wrong here?").
4. Send — the image is uploaded to Firebase Storage and analysed; LAPI replies based on what it reads from the image.

### Document upload

1. Tap **+** → **Upload Doc**.
2. Supported: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT.
3. Optionally add a question (e.g. "Summarise this document").
4. Send — the backend extracts text and LAPI answers your question about the file.

---

## Report Issue Button

The **Report Issue** button appears **only** when you tap the **"How to report an issue?"** suggestion card — not on every reply.

Tap it to go straight to the issue reporting form.

---

## Disruption Notice Cards

When you tap **"Check for water/power cut"**:

- If announcements match your profile area, you see one or more cards (water = cyan, power = amber, road = orange) with status, estimated restoration time, and reason.
- If none match, LAPI shows a short text message saying no disruptions were found.
- This session is saved to your chat history so you can view it later.

---

## Chat History

- Tap the **history icon** in the LAPI app bar to load past sessions into the current chat.
- Past messages show in order with date dividers between sessions.
- Images from past uploads are shown again when stored.
- Disruption cards are restored from saved history.

Each conversation is a **session**. Sessions appear in the history list with a short preview of the first message.

---

## Clear All History

Type **`/clear`** in the chat box (exact command, case-insensitive) and send.

This deletes **all** chat sessions linked to your user ID from Firestore and clears the current chat on screen. You will see a confirmation snackbar.

---

## What LAPI Can Help With

- How to report an issue in LaporFix (steps, photos, location, categories)
- General questions about urban complaints and public services
- Follow-up questions in the same chat session
- Summaries or questions about an uploaded document or image

## What LAPI Does Not Do

- Submit a report for you (use **Report Issue** or the (+) form)
- Guarantee real-time utility outage data (use **Check for water/power cut** or the Announcements page)
- Replace official government hotlines for emergencies

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Ask one clear question at a time.
- For reporting steps, use the **"How to report an issue?"** card or ask "How do I report a pothole?"
- For outages, use **"Check for water/power cut"** or check **Home → Announcements**.
- For ticket status, use **"Track my existing ticket"** or **My Reports** in the app.
- Set your home **area** in your profile so disruption and announcement filters work correctly.
